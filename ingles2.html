VOCABULARIO DE SÍNTOMAS Y ENFERMEDADES.

1 AN ALLERGY: a negative reaction to something you eat or touch.

Una alergia: una reacción negativa a algo que comes o tocas.

Juanita has a peanut allergy.

How about you take medicine?

2 ASTHMA: respiratory problem. Difficulty of breathing.

Asma: problema respiratorio. Dificultad para respirar.

Pedro has an asthma attack because he ran a lot.

He should use your inhaler.

3 A BACKACHE: a prolonged pain in the back

Dolor de espalda: un dolor prolongado en la espalda.

Luis carried something very heavy and that’s why he has back pain.

Why don’t you do some stretching?

4 A BROKEN LEG: a bone in the leg is broken.

Una pierna rota: se rompe un hueso de la pierna.

Julian broken his leg playing soccer.

He should go to the doctor.

5 A COUGH: the act of expelling air from the lungs with a sudden sharp sound.

Tos: el acto de expulsar el aire de los pulmones con un sonido agudo repentino.

Andrea has a cough because her throat is dry.

How about you drink water?

6 A COLD: a common viral infection which causes mucus to run from the nose, gives a sore throat and often includes sneezing.

Un resfriado: una infección viral común que hace que la mucosidad de la nariz, produce dolor de garganta y a menudo incluye estornudos.

Sara stayed in the rain for a long time and caught a cold.

She should have warm lemonade.

7 AN EARACHE: pain inside the ear.

Dolor de oído: dolor en el interior del oído.

Felipe has an earache because he listened to loud music.

Why don’t you reduce the volume a bit?

8 A FEVER: an abnormally high body temperature, usually accompanied by shivering and a headache.

Fiebre: una temperatura corporal anormalmente alta, generalmente acompañada de escalofríos y dolor de cabeza.

Maria got a fever because she has an infection.

How about you take a cold bath?

9 THE FLU: is the common name given for influenza. It is a contagious viral infection of the respiratory passages that causes fever and sever aching.

La gripe: es el nombre común que se le da a la influenza. Es una infección viral contagiosa de vías respiratorias que causa fiebre y dolor severo.

Mariano has the flu because he doesn’t cover himself well at night.

You should rest.

10 A HEADACHE: a continuous pain in the head.

Dolor de cabeza: dolor continuo en la cabeza.

Jhonatan got a headache because he hasn’t eaten.

Why don’t you eat better?

11 HEARTBURN: a form of indigestion felt as a burning sensation in the chest.

Acidez de estómago: una forma de indigestión que se siente como una sensación de ardor en el pecho.

Pablo has stomach upsets because he ate something very spicy.

How about you drink water with bicarbonate?

12 A RASH: a lot of small red spots on the skin that are usually itchy.

Una erupción: muchas pequeñas manchas rojas en la piel que generalmente pican.

Karol has a rash from touching poisonous Grass.

She should apply an ointment.

13 A SORE THROAT: a condition of pain in the throat, typically caused by inflammation of it.

Dolor de garganta: una condición de dolor en la garganta, generalmente causada por la inflamación de la misma.

Bianca has a sore throat because she yells a lot.

Why don’t you take a mint?

14 A SPRAIN: an injury to a joint in your body, especially your wrist or ankle, caused by sudden twisting.

Un esguince: una lesión en una articulación de su cuerpo, especialmente en la muñeca o en el tobillo, causada por una torcedura repentina.

Tatiana fell into one of her arms and sprained her.

How about you go to the hospital?

15 A STOMACHACHE: the pain in a person’s belly.

Dolor de estómago: el dolor en el vientre de una persona.

Camila ate a lot and gave her a stomachache.

She should drink lemon water.

16 A TOOTHACHE: the pain in a tooth or teeth.

Dolor de muelas: el dolor en una muela o muelas.

Fabian has a toothache because he doesn’t brush well.

He should go to the dentist.
